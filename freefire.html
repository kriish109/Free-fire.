Welcome to Free FireFan Page

>Free Fire is a free-to-play first-person tactical shooter by Garena. Released in 2018, the game has captured the attention of millions of players worldwide.

About Free Fire

Free Fire combines tactical gunplay with unique character abilities. It requires a combination of strategy, teamwork, and precise aiming to succeed.

Gameplay Modes

Free Fire offers a variety of exciting game modes to enjoy:

Classic bermuda

The standard 50 player mode where teams compete to kill all the players and get the Booyah!.

RANK GAME

Like Bermuda, but with a ranked system that tests your skill level.

Lone wolf

A faster-paced, shorter match format with random weapon loadouts.

Clash squard

Pure combat to 4 v 4 your aim and to get a Booyah in this also.
